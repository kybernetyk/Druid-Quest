Druid Quest is a fascinating logic puzzle game.

The arch druid is lost and must find back to his village. And he needs your help to find his way through 25 mind-boggling levels.

Druid Quest offers:

- 25 puzzling levels
- beautiful hand drawn graphics
- a gripping sound track
- a world wide high scores list
- free level packs delivered every few weeks
- fun for everyone ages 9 to 99
